What's it like at CSH?

Nothing but passion and hard work.

Projects, Projects, Projects!

Members set out to learn new things every day, and apply those awesome things that they learned to new and interesting projects. From your very first day as a freshman at CSH, we encourage everyone to work on projects outside of class!

Outdoor Adventures!

From the day you arrive at RIT for the fall semester, we spend plenty of time outdoors. From out annual Fall Camping Trip to our own private campsite to BBQ's in the quad, there's nothing like hanging out and taking a break in the sun.

Friends for Life!

With alumni going back 40 years and counting, members are bound to make plenty of friends and have nothing but a great time with our incredible community of CSH'ers.

Company Visits!

Throughout the year, tech giants like Google, Apple, and Microsoft all line up to give our members some awesome tech talks on stuff they're working on. Members even have the opportunity to hand their resume directly to employers instead of waiting in line at the career fair!

It's not all
about computers.

While CSH was certainly started with the purpose of bring people together who loved computers, today it's about connecting people who are passionate about anything in order to create incredible things, and push forward the next generation of incredible thinkers.

Solving Real Problems

Our members always aim to see what problems can be solved, and make the next big thing bigger.

Live n' Learn

Every member is given the tools and resources to learn whatever skills they want, no matter what your major is.
